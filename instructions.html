Filter by Restaurant

To search for a restaurant in our database, follow the steps below!

  1. Click on the "Filter" button.
  2. Select "Restaurant Name" to start searching for restaurants.
  3. Ensure that "contains" is selected as the operator
  4. Type in the name of the restaurant you're looking for.
  5. Browse through all the menu offerings for your desired restaurant!
